Historic Moshoeshoe Walk Concluded with Celebration.

The historic three-day Moshoeshoe Walk, covering a distance of approximately 116 km, came to triumphant end on Saturday eveming. The walk had a diverse group pf local and international tourists participating in a journey that honored the legacy of the founding father of Lesotho, King Moshoeshoe 1.
